## 🌟 Transform Minecraft into an MMORPG!

Wonderlands is a comprehensive **quality of life** modpack that transforms Minecraft into a full MMORPG experience with fantasy elements, custom classes, dungeons, and hundreds of new features.

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎮 MMORPG Gameplay
- **4 Unique Classes**: Warrior, Mage, Archer, Assassin with skill trees
- **Leveling System**: Gain XP, unlock abilities, become more powerful
- **Party System**: Team up with friends for dungeons

### 🏰 Dungeons & Bosses
- **25+ Handcrafted Dungeons** with increasing difficulty
- **40+ New Bosses** with unique mechanics and loot tables
- **Procedural Generation** for endless replayability

### 👹 New Content
- **150+ New Items**: Weapons, armor, artifacts, consumables
- **40+ New Mobs**: Dragons, elementals, undead, mythical creatures
- **Custom Structures**: Towers, camps, shrines, hidden temples

### ⚡ Quality of Life
- **Backpacks & Bags** for extra inventory
- **Minimap & Waypoints** for navigation
- **Inventory Sorting** and quick stack features
- **JEI Integration** for crafting recipes
- **Better HUD** with status effects and cooldowns

### 🎨 Visual & Audio
- **Custom Particle Effects** for spells and abilities
- **Dynamic Lighting** for torches and spells
- **Original Soundtrack** for biomes and boss fights
- **Shader Compatibility** for enhanced graphics
